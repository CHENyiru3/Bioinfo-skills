# Statistical Validity Policy

Status: draft

The system must prevent method names and result claims from exceeding what the
input state and statistical design support.

## Marker Ranking

Cluster marker ranking is descriptive. It must not be described as
replicate-aware condition-level differential expression.

## Pseudobulk Differential Expression

Condition-level DE requires sample-level replication, raw integer counts, a
valid design, and declared contrasts.

## Annotation

Annotation outputs are candidate labels until approved by a human reviewer.

## Signature Scoring

Signature scores are relative summaries. They are not causal pathway activity
proof without supporting model and evidence.
